## 2025 Toyota Tacoma Double Cab Overview

### General Information
- **Seating Capacity**: 5
- **Cargo Space**: Available with 5.0-foot or 6.1-foot bed options

### MSRP and Trims
The 2025 Toyota Tacoma Double Cab is available in several trims, each with its own set of features and pricing:

1. **SR**
   - **MSRP**: $33,790
   - **Key Features**:
     - 2.4L 4-cylinder turbo engine
     - 17-inch steel wheels
     - LED headlights
     - Composite cargo bed
     - Class IV trailer-hitch receiver
     - 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system
     - Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
     - Toyota Safety Sense 3.0

2. **SR5**
   - **MSRP**: $37,290
   - **Key Features**:
     - All features of the SR trim
     - 17-inch alloy wheels
     - Bed rails with movable tie-down cleats
     - Keyless entry on all doors
     - Blind-spot warning with rear cross-traffic warning

3. **TRD Sport**
   - **MSRP**: $39,400
   - **Key Features**:
     - All features of the SR5 trim
     - 18-inch alloy wheels
     - Sport-tuned suspension
     - Hood scoop
     - Wireless charging pad
     - 10-way power-adjustable driver's seat

4. **TRD Off-Road**
   - **MSRP**: $42,900
   - **Key Features**:
     - All features of the TRD Sport trim
     - Off-road suspension with Bilstein shocks
     - Locking rear differential
     - Multi-terrain select and crawl control

5. **Limited**
   - **MSRP**: $52,555
   - **Key Features**:
     - All features of the TRD Off-Road trim
     - 20-inch alloy wheels
     - Leather-trimmed seats
     - Premium JBL audio system
     - Panoramic view monitor

6. **TRD Pro**
   - **MSRP**: $63,735
   - **Key Features**:
     - All features of the Limited trim
     - 2.4L turbo hybrid engine
     - FOX internal bypass shocks
     - TRD Pro skid plates
     - Unique TRD Pro styling

### Performance
- **Engine**: 2.4L 4-cylinder turbo (standard), 2.4L turbo hybrid (TRD Pro)
- **Horsepower**: Up to 278 hp (standard), up to 326 hp (hybrid)
- **Transmission**: 8-speed automatic or 6-speed manual (select trims)
- **Drive Type**: Rear-wheel drive (RWD) or Four-wheel drive (4WD)
- **Fuel Economy**: 
  - **City**: 19 MPG
  - **Highway**: 23 MPG
  - **Combined**: 21 MPG

### Dimensions
- **Length**: 212.3 inches (5.0-foot bed), 225.5 inches (6.1-foot bed)
- **Width**: 75.2 inches
- **Height**: 70.6 inches
- **Wheelbase**: 127.4 inches

### Safety Features
- Forward collision warning with automatic braking
- Adaptive cruise control
- Lane departure mitigation
- Lane centering system
- Blind-spot warning with rear cross-traffic warning (SR5 and above)
- Stability control
- Traction control

The 2025 Toyota Tacoma Double Cab offers a blend of rugged capability, advanced safety features, and modern technology, making it a versatile choice for those needing a reliable midsize pickup[1](https://www.toyota.com/tacoma/features/)[2](https://www.edmunds.com/toyota/tacoma/2025/features-specs/)[3](https://pressroom.toyota.com/the-2025-toyota-tacoma-is-ready-for-adventure/).
